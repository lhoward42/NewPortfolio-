About

I am a Full Stack Web Developer and recent graduate of ElevenFifty Academy. My focus is creating user-friendly web applications using JavaScript. I am passionate about breaking things down into smaller steps in order to build quality high-performing applications.

In 2014, after finishing my Bachelor in Sociology at Purdue University I landed my first career opportunity in sales as a Mortgage Broker with Royal United Mortgage before pursuing a college passion of becoming a bartender and landed a position with Metro NightClub, a high volume night club and restaurant. I eventually moved up to a senior bartender role all while progressively pursuing music professionally on my off hours. As a musical artist I deal with my fair share of musical equipment and software, the more you just fall in love with the process the more you become interested in the technology going behind it and how it's making the vision come to life for everyone else to experience and that became my pursuit for more. As with the most of the world, the pandemic halted a lot when it came to my bartending and musical career and I utilized that time to began my research and making career decisions. I spent time investing and building up my home studio in quarantine, researching and comparing equipment, putting it together, getting some music recorded, and having conversations with one of my producer and tech writing friends, Hasan, where I began to research careers in tech and became interested in how building websites and applications are built and how I could make this a career path. Not long after that I came across a couple of bootcamps and I decided to enroll in ElevenFifty Academy's Web Development program. It was an immersive program where we learned Full Stack Development with a focus on HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Typescript, and many other technologies.
