About

I am a Full Stack Web Developer and recent graduate of ElevenFifty Academy. I am passionate about simplicity and efficiency when approaching complex problems. I enjoy creating user-friendly web applications using JavaScript.

In 2014, I finished my Bachelor in Sociology at Purdue University then started my career in sales and customer service in both the Mortgage industry as a Mortgage Broker before moving on to work 5 years as a bartender being promoted to senior bartender by year 2 at Metro NightCub all while pursuing my passion in music and music technology. As a musical artist who deals with my fair share of musical equipment and software, I've always loved technology and knew I wanted to pursue some sort of technical career aside from music, especially with all of the influences from my peers at Purdue pursing various technical and engineering degrees. This last year (2020-2021) spending seriously building up my home studio in quarantine, getting some tunes recorded, and doing more research in the technical world of music led me to want to learn how to build web apps so I could help myself, my friends, and have a career skill outside of music that I am also equally passionate about.
