About

I am a Full Stack Web Developer and recent graduate of ElevenFifty Academy. I am passionate about simplicity and efficiency when approaching complex problems. My focus is creating user-friendly web applications using JavaScript.

In 2014, after finishing my Bachelor in Sociology at Purdue University I jump started my career in sales and customer service in both the Mortgage industry as a Mortgage Broker before moving on to working 2 years as a server/bartender later being promoted to senior bartender for 3 years at serving my entire Metro NightClub, a high volume night club and restaurant, all while pursuing my passion/hardcore professional hobby in music. As a musical artist I deal with my fair share of musical equipment and software, and sometimes as you fall more in love with the process you become more engrained in the technology and that became my pursuit for more. The pandemic changed a lot for my bartending and musical career and I utilized that time to began my research and making career decisions. I spent time investing and building up my home studio in quarantine, getting some tunes recorded, and all while consulting my producer and tech writing friend, Hasan which led me to want to learn how to begin a career in Web Development and so I enrolled in ElevenFifty Academy's bootcamp program and it gave me the guidance and tools to learn and opened my eyes to the grit and dedication it takes to be a programmer.
